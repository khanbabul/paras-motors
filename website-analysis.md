# Paras Motor Werk Shop - Website Analysis

## Reference Website Analysis (motorwerksgroup.com)

### Design Elements
- Clean, professional automotive theme
- Dark header with luxury car background image
- Navigation: About, Services, Gallery, Inventory, Contact
- Contact info prominently displayed in header
- Business hours visible
- Logo with automotive styling

### Color Scheme
- Dark header/navigation
- White/light background for content
- Professional automotive color palette

### Key Sections
1. **Header**: Logo, navigation, contact info, business hours
2. **Hero Section**: Large automotive image background
3. **About Section**: Company specialization and services overview
4. **Services Section**: 
   - Vehicle Repair & Maintenance
   - Collision, Paint & Body Center
   - Luxury Vehicle Restoration
   - High Performance Tuning
   - Wheel and Tire Specialists
   - Alignment Services
5. **Why Choose Us**: Value proposition
6. **Contact**: Phone number prominently displayed
7. **Footer**: Copyright and attribution

### Content Focus
- Luxury sports cars (Mercedes-Benz, BMW, Audi, Porsche, Ferrari, Lamborghini, VW, Bentley)
- Complete automotive services under one roof
- Professional expertise and equipment
- Competitive pricing vs dealers

### Adaptation for Paras Motor Werk Shop
- Maintain professional automotive theme
- Similar navigation structure
- Adapt content for "Paras Motor Werk Shop" branding
- Keep focus on luxury automotive services
- Professional, trustworthy presentation
